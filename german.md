## Pronouns

### Personal

| Per | Singular    | Pl  |
|-----|-------------|-----|
| 1st | Ich         | Wir |
| 2nd | Du          | Ihr |
| 3rd | Er, es, sie | Sie |

### Posessive

| Per | Singular    | Plur  |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| 1st | mein        | unser |
| 2nd | dein        | euer  |
| 3rd | sein, ihr   | ihr   |

Endings are added like the endings of *ein*. *Unser* and *euer* have the stems *unsr-* and *eur-* respectively.

Seine - his
mein - my
dein - your
